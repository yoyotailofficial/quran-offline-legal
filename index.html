Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Quran Audio — Listen Offline app ("the App") handles information. In short: the App does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No account or sign-up is required or available.
  • No name, email, phone number, or contact list is collected.
  • No advertising identifiers are collected — the App shows no ads.
  • No analytics or usage-tracking SDKs are embedded in the App.
  • No location data is accessed or collected.

What stays on your device

Your preferences (theme, default reciter, download settings) and your list of downloaded surahs are stored locally on your device only, using standard Android app storage. This information is never transmitted to us or to any third party, and is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App.

Network requests

To fetch surah/reciter information and to stream or download recitation audio, the App connects directly to the public API and audio servers of mp3quran.net. These requests include standard technical information (such as your IP address) that any network request contains, as is the case with any app that connects to the internet. We do not operate our own backend server and do not see or store this traffic ourselves.

Permissions used

PermissionWhy it's needed
InternetTo fetch surah/reciter data and audio.

Foreground Service (Data Sync)To download surahs for offline listening.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information from any user.

Disclaimer

Independent, unofficial app

Quran Audio — Listen Offline is an independent app created to make Qur'an recitation easy to listen to, including offline. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to any of the featured reciters, mp3quran.net, or any religious authority or organization.

Source of content

All recitation audio and reciter/surah metadata are retrieved from the public, freely-provided API of mp3quran.net. The App does not host, alter, or re-encode this audio — it plays and, when you choose to download, stores an unmodified copy of the file provided by that service.

No claim of authoritative accuracy

While every reasonable effort is made to present recitations and surah information correctly, the App makes no guarantee of absolute accuracy, completeness, or fitness for religious rulings (fatwa) or scholarly reference. For matters of religious guidance, please consult a qualified scholar or an authoritative mushaf/source.

Availability

Because audio is streamed from a third-party service, availability of specific reciters or surahs may occasionally change or become temporarily unavailable due to circumstances outside the App's control.

Non-commercial, da'wah intent

This App is built and offered free of charge, with no advertisements, no in-app purchases, and no monetization, as a form of da'wah (Islamic outreach) intended purely for the benefit of listeners, in sha Allah.
